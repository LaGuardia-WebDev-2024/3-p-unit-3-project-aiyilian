Stir Fry Bok Choy

  • Time: 20 minutes
  • Serves: 4 people

Step 1: Wash bok choy thoroughly

  • Peel away each individual stem, and toss them into a bowl of large water
  • Scrub your fingers along the bottom of each stem closest to the root. Dirt usually accumulates here. Rinse under running water.
  • If you have time, soak the bok choy in water for 30 minutes

Step 2: Dry and trim bok choy

  • Drain the bok choy (1 lb) in a colander, and lay them flat onto a towel. It's important that the bok choy is dry before stir-frying.
  • Trim the bok choy by cutting the stalks in half length-wise.
  • It is optional to seperate the stalks from the leaves, some people prefer to cook the stalks first then the leaves

Step 3: Mince garlic and heat wok and oil

  • Heat the wok on high heat for 2-3 minutes
  • While the wok heats up, mince some garlic (2 cloves)
  • Then, add oil (2 tbsp) until the oil is shimmering, or forming ripples across the surface. It's important that the wok and oil get sufficiently hot.

Step 4: Stir fry garlic and bok choy

  • Add the minced garlic,add the bok choy a few seconds later.
  • Cook everything for 6-8 minutes or until the stalks soften up and the leaves turn a deep vibrant green

Step 5: Season and plate

  • Right before the heat turns off,add salt (0.50 tsp), sugar (0.50 tsp), and chicken bouillon (0.50 tsp) (optional).
  • To stop the bok choy from overcooking, transfer them onto a plate immediately.

Egg Fried Rice

Step 1: Wash and cook rice

  • Wash the rice (14 oz) three times, using fresh water after each rinse, drain the rice thoroughly after the final rinse
  • Add rice, followed by the water (14 oz) to a rice cooker and cook according to rice cooker instructions
  • If you want the restraunt style fried rice texture, it is reccomended to use day old refridgerated rice

Step 2: Cut green onions and carrot

  • Cut the green parts off of the green onions (1 oz) and dice into small pieces, save the whites to use in another dish
  • Dice the carrot into small pieces
  • Combine carrots with the frozen corn and peas (4 oz) in bowl

Step 3: Crack and season eggs

  • Crack the eggs (4 large) into a bowl
  • Season with the salt (0.50 teaspoon), then set aside

Step 4: Microwave veggies and fluff rice

  • Once the rice is done cooking, leave it covered to steam for 2 additional minutes
  • add the water (0.50 cup) to the bowl of vegetables and microwave for 2 minutes to cook. Drain out the excess water and set aside
  • Use chopsticks to stir and fluff rice for 1 to 2 minutes.

Step 5: Stir fry and season

  • Heat the wok on high and beat the eggs, lower the heat to low and add the oil (2 tablespoon), spread it across the wok surface to make sure it's well coated
  • Add eggs and stir gently, cook them for about 20 to 30 seconds, add the cooked rice on top of the eggs, flip and stir fry constantly, breaking apart the clumps of rice and eggs with a wok spatula for about 2 minutes
  • Add mixed veggies, increase the heat to high, flip and stir fry for another 2 to 3 minutes
  • In a small bowl, combine light soy sauce (1 tablespoon), dark soy sauce (1 teaspoon), salt (0.50 teaspoon), sugar (0.50 teaspoon), and chicken bouillon powder (1 teaspoon), and pour over the rice
  • Increase the heat to high and stir fry everything for another 2 to 3 minutes
  • Add the oil (1 tablespoon) and diced green onions, stir fry for another minute , then turn the heat off and plate
